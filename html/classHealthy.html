Examines the plant's condition and transitions to appropriate states. Checks if the plant needs water, fertilizer, or pruning and transitions accordingly:

  • To Dehydrated if water is needed
  • To Malnurished if fertilizer is needed
  • To OverGrown if pruning is needed
